Draw 4 Med Website

I created this website for a nonprofit. Children can submit their medical stories along with some artwork to inspire and share their voices to others. For this architecture, I used Google App Scripts, Google Sheets, Firebase, and HTML/CSS/JS. This was useful because we could control all data from the Google sheets rather than the code.

It had over 600 users, and 2600 page views
